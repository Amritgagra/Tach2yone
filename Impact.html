ESG integration

ESG integration in the investment process

During the analysis of each investment opportunity, our team checks that the company is compatible with the exclusion policy, analyzes the contribution to SDGs and carries out a sustainability risk analysis. The results of the ESG due diligence is included in the presentation to the investment committee for each new investment.

Our commitment

Sustainable and Responsible

Tach2yone is a signatory of the United Nations Principles for Responsible Investment (UN PRI). Our commitment and approach to ESG are summarized in a Sustainability and Responsible Investment policy.

Positive impact

Making a difference

By financing Green Tech companies, Tach2yone contributes to creating a positive impact on society measured by the contributions to the United Nations Sustainable Development Goals As a management company, Tach2yone’s commitment is to invest its subscribers’ funds in accordance with its commitments to Responsible investment.
